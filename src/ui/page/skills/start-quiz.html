Measure your skills quiz

This quiz is based on the policy profession standards. The standards are grouped into the following three themes: Evidence, Politics and Delivery. Each theme has six skill areas.

You can choose a short quiz (around 10 minutes) as a taster, or the full quiz (around 30 minutes) if you want to get more detailed feedback. You can also customise your own quiz to include specificcomponents tailored to your needs.

This tool will help you to:

  • assess your skills at an awareness level
  • find learning to learn more based on your result
  • share the result with your manager (optional)
