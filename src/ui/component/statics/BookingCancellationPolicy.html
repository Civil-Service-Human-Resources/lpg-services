Cancellation policy

If you wish to cancel your booking, the policy is:

  • For cancellations 16 working days or more before the commencement date, the buyer/booker/customer will not be charged.
  • For cancellations 11 to 15 working days before the commencement date, the buyer/booker/customer will be charged 30% of the total cost.
  • For cancellations less than 11 working days before the commencement date, the buyer/booker/customer will be charged 100% of the total cost.

Cancellation policy in full

If you wish to cancel your booking, the policy is:

  • For cancellations 16 working days or more before the commencement date, the buyer/booker/customer will not be charged.
  • For cancellations 11 to 15 working days before the commencement date, the buyer/booker/customer will be charged 30% of the total cost.
  • For cancellations less than 11 working days before the commencement date, the buyer/booker/customer will be charged 100% of the total cost.

The ‘days’ referred to are actual days rather than working days.

The ‘opening’ of the course is the date on which any digital learning or preparatory work is released and made available to you.

We reserve the right to cancel a course if the minimum number of participants has not been reached.

Please ensure that the information you provide for your booking is valid and accurate. Inaccurate booking information may result in your booking being cancelled or your department incurring costs.
