If you wish to cancel your participation in a course, you may have to pay a cancellation charge. The ‘days’ referred to in the cancellation policy are actual days rather than working days. The commencement of the course is the date on which any digital learning or pre-workshop work is released and made available to you.

Please ensure that the information you provide for your booking is valid and accurate. Inaccurate booking information may result in your booking being cancelled or may result in your department incurring costs.

The cancellation policy is:

  • 22 days or more before the commencement of a course - no charge
  • 14 to 21 days before the commencement of a course – you will be charged 30% of the total course cost
  • Less than 14 days before the commencement of a course you will be charged 100% of the of the total course cost.

We reserve the right to cancel a course if minimum delegate numbers have not been reached.

  • You may access the digital course materials yourself as many times as you wish.
